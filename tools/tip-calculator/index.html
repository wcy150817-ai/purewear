How to Split Tips Fairly

The easiest way to split tips is equally among all team members who worked the shift. This calculator helps you divide the total tip amount instantly.

Note: Some teams prefer to split based on hours worked. For now, this calculator supports equal splitting.

💡 Pro Tip

Make sure to keep a record of tip distributions for tax purposes. Tips are considered income by the IRS.
