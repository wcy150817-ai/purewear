How Overtime Works

In many places, overtime pay is required by law for hours worked over 40 per week. The standard overtime rate is 1.5 times (time and a half) your regular hourly rate.

Example: If your rate is $20/hour, your overtime rate would be $30/hour ($20 x 1.5).

💡 Tip

Don't forget to track your tips! Tips are income, but usually calculated separately for tax purposes.
