How to Use Dilution Ratios

Most cleaning chemicals use ratios like 1:10 or 1:4. This means 1 part chemical mixed with X parts water.

Example: If you need 32 oz total and the ratio is 1:10, you will mix approx 2.9 oz of product with 29.1 oz of water.

⚠️ Safety First

Always check the product label. Using too much chemical can damage surfaces and be unsafe.
