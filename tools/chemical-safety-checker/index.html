⚠️ General Safety Rule

Never mix different cleaning products unless you are sure they are compatible. Mixing bleach with ammonia or acids creates deadly gases. Always read the label on the bottle.

☠️ DANGER

Mixing bleach with vinegar or ammonia creates toxic gas that can hurt your lungs or even kill you.
