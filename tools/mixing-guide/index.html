📖 How to Use

Method 1 - With Scale Marks:
If your bottle or bucket has measurement marks (like 100ml, 200ml), choose this method. We'll tell you exactly where to fill to.

Method 2 - No Scale Marks:
If your container has no measurements, just use any cup. Fill your container with water using that cup, count how many cups it takes, then we'll tell you how many cups of chemical to add.

What does 1:20 mean?
It means 1 part chemical + 20 parts water. For example: 1 cup chemical + 20 cups water.

💡 Pro Tip

Always add chemical to water, not water to chemical. This prevents splashing concentrated solution.

⚠️ Check Label

Every product is different. Always double-check the label on your specific bottle. This tool provides general guidance.
