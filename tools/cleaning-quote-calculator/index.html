How to Calculate Cleaning Costs?

Cleaning costs are typically calculated based on square footage. The average rate for standard cleaning ranges from $0.10 to $0.15 per square foot. Deep cleaning services, which include detailed scrubbing of bathrooms and kitchens, can cost up to $0.18 per square foot.

This calculator helps housekeepers and cleaning business owners quickly generate quotes. It factors in base rates, service types, and common add-on services like cleaning inside appliances.

💡 Pro Tip

Regular clients often get discounts! Consider offering weekly or bi-weekly rates to build long-term relationships.
